My projects

Extended Roadtrip Northway

CO-OP RV Survival game in Godot.

ERN is a survival game in which the player tries to cooperate with other people to survive the harsh snowy environment by scavenging the land for resources while driving and maintaining their RV.

The game is currently in prototyping phase.

Project Explore

TPP 2.5D survival game made in Godot.

Predecessor of Extended Roadtrip Northway. The project was mostly focused on creating a moldy broken world that came after a collapse of a society that once stood there.
The events of the game took place in ruins of a city standing on top of a mega structure from thausands of years ago. This mage structure was used to extract souls from an anomalies known as "Soul Wells" which then powered the city standing above it without realizing they were slowly enraging the wells and causing an even known as "The Collapse" leaving nothing but 1 survivor of the event.

The player's goal was to explore the world and figure out what happened to the land while avoiding the original survivor of The Collapse which caused them to slowly turn into a creature barely recognizable as human on the outside, but still alive on the inside.

Project Soul

TPP rogue-like which I've made as my graduation project in Unity.

This project features inventory mechanic with looting, combat, boss-fights, and a tiny bit of lore. The graphics aren't anything special, I'm a programmer, not an artist.

The game is no longer getting any updates as I've successfully graduated in May of 2024.

Explorer's Gear

Minecraft mod that attempts to make exploration more fun and enjoyable by adding items themed around travelling.

Exploration is one of my favorite parts of any video game experience. Exploring enviroments generated or designed by developers has some magic feeling to it, and Minecraft is no exception to this. Unfortunately exploration in Minecraft isn't exactly the most engaging experience, so that's why I wanted to make this mod.

Darkest Stress

Minecraft mod that adds stress.

I was obsessed with Darkest Dungeon and Thaumcraft at the time an. The player can gain stress by spending time in dark places, not eating or eating bad/unhealthy food. On the other hand the player can reduce their stress be eating better meals and sleeping. Upon hitting a certain stress level the player will roll for an affliction, similarly to Darkest Dungeon, which can be positive or negative.

Risk of Math

Web application for calculating Risk of Rain 2 item effects for a school project.

Basically, I used to play a lot of Risk of Rain 2 and I wanted an easy way to calculate statistics of items and I didn't know that you can mod the game back then, so I made a stat calculator for most of the items and playable characters in JavaScript.
