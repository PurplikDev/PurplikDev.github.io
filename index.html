Current Project

Project Soul

Project Soul is a 3rd person dungeon crawler that I am working on.

I started working on this game as a passion project that I wanted to do in my free time when I didn't feel like modding but it eventually became a project for my graduation.

The main idea of this game is that the player is rediscovering ancient magic called "Soul bending". The player will have research these magical arts and shape them to their liking to utilize their powers in a dark dungeon full of beasts, monsters and other creatures that should remain hidden in the depths.
