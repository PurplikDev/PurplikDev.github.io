My projects

Krtk!

Krtk is a PvP party game made in Godot.

It's a game I am currently creating with few of my friends as a passion project with hopes of starting a small Indie Game Development Studio. It's a game where you play as a mole and you want to kill the other players to be the last mole standing.

The game is still very early in development.

Hats & Cosmetics

This mod adds new equippable cosmetic items into the game that the player can put on their character.

My favorite part of video games is when you can make your character look very stylish and dapper. Unfortunately the customization Minecraft provides isn't stylish enough for me, so that's why I made Hats & Cosmetics.

This project has over 120k downloads in total which I am very thankful for!

Project page

Explorer's Gear

This mod attempts to make exploration more fun and enjoyable by adding items themed around travelling.

Exploration is one of my favorite parts of any video game experience. Exploring enviroments generated or designed by developers has some magic feeling to it, and Minecraft is no exception to this. Unfortunately exploration in Minecraft isn't exactly the most engaging experience, so that's why I wanted to make this mod.

Project page

Risk of Math

This is a web application that I made as part of my school project.

Basically, I used to play a lot of Risk of Rain 2 and I wanted an easy way to calculate statistics of items and I didn't know that you can mod the game back then, so I made a stat calculator for most of the items and playable characters in JavaScript.
